═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    LEAVE TYPES & LIMITS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. SICK LEAVE
   - Limit: 6 days per year
   - Resets: Annually (January 1st)
   - Color: Blue (Info)

2. CASUAL LEAVE
   - Limit: 6 days per year
   - Resets: Annually (January 1st)
   - Color: Green (Success)

3. EARNED LEAVE
   - Limit: 6 days per year
   - Resets: Annually (January 1st)
   - Color: Primary Blue

4. MENSTRUAL LEAVE (Female Employees Only)
   - Limit: 12 days per year (1 per month)
   - Resets: Annually (January 1st)
   - Color: Yellow (Warning)
   - Visibility: Only shown to female employees
   - Gender Check: Based on EmployeeProfile.gender field

5. UNPAID LEAVE
   - Limit: Unlimited
   - No balance tracking
   - Always available

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LEAVE BALANCE CALCULATION:
- Counts approved leaves for current year
- Shows: Used / Total
- Progress bar shows remaining percentage
- Updates in real-time when leaves are approved

MENSTRUAL LEAVE LOGIC:
1. Check if employee has EmployeeProfile
2. Check if gender field exists and equals 'female' (case-insensitive)
3. If female: Show menstrual leave option and balance
4. If male or gender not set: Hide menstrual leave completely

ANNUAL RESET:
- All balances reset on January 1st
- Previous year's leaves don't carry forward
- Fresh allocation each year

LEAVE REQUEST PAGE:

FOR MALE EMPLOYEES:

⚠️ GENDER FIELD REQUIREMENT:
- Menstrual leave visibility depends on EmployeeProfile.gender
- Ensure all female employees have gender set to 'female'
- Case-insensitive check (Female, female, FEMALE all work)
- If gender not set, menstrual leave won't show

⚠️ BALANCE ENFORCEMENT:
- System shows balance but doesn't prevent over-limit requests
- HR can still approve leaves beyond limit if needed
- Balance is informational, not restrictive
- HR has final approval authority

⚠️ ANNUAL RESET:
- Balances reset on January 1st automatically
- Based on current year in leave calculation
- No manual reset needed
