================================================================================
VALIDATION RULES
================================================================================

Employee ID:
- Unique
- Cannot be changed after creation

Email:
- Must be unique
- Valid email format
- Domain validation (optional)

Date of Birth:
- Valid date format
- Used for registration validation

Phone Number:
- Exactly 10 digits
- Numbers only

Aadhaar Number:
- Exactly 12 digits
- Numbers only, no spaces

PAN Number:
- Exactly 10 characters
- Format: ABCDE1234F (5 letters, 4 numbers, 1 letter)
- Auto-converts to uppercase

Blood Group:
- Must be one of: A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, AB-, O+, O-

Gender:
- Must be: male, female, or other

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

ERROR: "no such table: attendance_employeemasterdata"
SOLUTION: Run python manage.py migrate

ERROR: "Details Entered Are Not Within Our Organization"
SOLUTION: 
- Check Employee ID exists in master data
- Check Email matches exactly (case-insensitive)
- Check Date of Birth matches exactly

ERROR: "An account already exists for this Employee ID"
SOLUTION: Employee has already registered. Use password reset if needed.

ERROR: CSV upload fails
SOLUTION:
- Check date format is DD-MMM-YYYY
- Check all required columns are present
- Check column names match exactly
- Check data format (phone 10 digits, PAN format, etc.)

ERROR: Cannot edit profile fields
SOLUTION: Master data fields are read-only for employees. Contact HR to update.
