IMPROVEMENT 2: HR AND ADMIN MERGED
================================================================================

CHANGED:
--------
1. Removed separate "Admin" role
2. Combined into single "HR/Admin" role
3. Both have identical full system access

ROLE OPTIONS NOW:
-----------------
1. Employee - Basic attendance features
2. Team Leader - Manage team & approve leaves  
3. HR/Admin - Full system access

PERMISSIONS:
------------
Employee:
- Check in/out
- Take breaks
- Request leaves
- View own attendance

Team Leader:
- All employee features
- View team attendance
- Approve team leaves
- Team reports

HR/Admin (Same access):
- All team leader features
- Manage all employees
- Edit attendance status
- Master data management
- Reset passwords
- Change roles
- Full system access
- All reports
